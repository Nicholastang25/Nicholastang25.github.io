Our Mission

As seniors, we face the pivotal decision of pursuing higher education, taking a break, or launching our careers through internships or jobs. Our goal is to effortlessly discover relevant job opportunities that align with our experiences and interests. We aim to design and develop a web application that simplifies the resume creation process, streamlines job searches, and offers user-friendly interview preparation. Our solution is designed to save users time and ensure that they present their best selves to potential employers.
